# Pengenalan kepada Penyiasatan Saintifik — Teaching Notes

## Overview
Pengenalan kepada penyiasatan saintifik adalah asas kepada pemahaman bagaimana saintis bekerja. Ia melibatkan pemahaman tentang konsep sains dalam kehidupan seharian, penggunaan alat dan unit pengukuran, serta kemahiran proses sains yang penting dalam menjalankan penyiasatan saintifik.

## Prerequisites Check
Students should have a basic understanding of what science is and how it affects their daily lives. Familiarity with basic mathematical operations and some prior exposure to laboratory environments will be beneficial.

## Teaching Sequence & Strategy

### 1. Menghubungkait Aktiviti Seharian dengan Sains (15 min)
Introduce students to examples of daily activities that involve science, such as cooking, using electronic devices, and transportation.
- **Strategies:** Use storytelling and real-life examples to illustrate points.
- **Check for Understanding (CFU):** Ask students to share examples of how they see science in their daily activities.
- **The Trap:** Students might think science is only about laboratory experiments. Emphasize its presence everywhere.

### 2. Memerihalkan Bidang Sains (20 min)
Discuss different fields of science and their importance in everyday life.
- **Strategies:** Use visual aids like charts and videos to explain various science fields.
- **Check for Understanding (CFU):** Ask students to identify which field of science interests them the most and why.
- **The Trap:** Potential confusion between science and technology. Clarify the differences and interplay between them.

## High Alert Misconceptions
| Misconception | Why Students Think This | How to Fix |
|---------------|-------------------------|------------|
| Science is only about experiments. | Experiments are often highlighted in media. | Provide examples of theoretical science. |
| All scientific careers require working in a lab. | Media portrayal of scientists in lab settings. | Discuss diverse science-related careers. |

## Engagement Hooks
- Discuss how local agricultural practices use scientific principles.
- Highlight innovations in technology developed in Malaysia.

## Assessment Guidance
Encourage students to present their understanding through projects that relate science to their daily lives. Assess their ability to connect theory with practice and evaluate their communication of scientific ideas.
