# Teknik Mengukur Parameter Kesihatan Badan — Teaching Notes

## Overview
This topic covers essential skills in measuring various health parameters including body temperature, pulse rate, blood pressure, and Body Mass Index (BMI). Students will learn to apply techniques, interpret results, and make informed decisions based on health data.

## Prerequisites Check
Students should have a basic understanding of human anatomy, specifically where major organs and blood vessels are located. Familiarity with basic mathematical operations and units of measurement is also necessary.

## Teaching Sequence & Strategy

### 1. Mengukur Suhu Badan (15 min)
Introduce the tools used for measuring body temperature, such as digital thermometers. Demonstrate the correct method of using these tools.

- **Strategies:** Use real-life thermometers, videos for visual demonstration
- **Check for Understanding (CFU):** "Can you show me how to use a thermometer?"
- **The Trap:** Students may not wait long enough for an accurate reading.

### 2. Menginterpretasi Suhu Badan (10 min)
Discuss normal body temperature ranges and what deviations might indicate.

- **Strategies:** Charts showing normal vs. fever temperatures
- **Check for Understanding (CFU):** "What does a temperature of 38°C suggest?"
- **The Trap:** Confusing Celsius with Fahrenheit scales.

## High Alert Misconceptions
| Misconception | Why Students Think This | How to Fix |
|---------------|-------------------------|------------|
| Pulse can be felt anywhere | Lack of understanding of circulatory system | Use diagrams to show specific pulse points |

## Engagement Hooks
- Discuss common situations where knowing how to measure body temperature is crucial, like during a fever or in a clinical setting.

## Assessment Guidance
When assessing students, focus on their ability to perform measurements accurately and their understanding of what the readings mean. Provide scenarios for them to interpret.
